Klaviyo now enables personalized product recommendations in SMS and RCS messages, giving brands the ability to send each subscriber a different, dynamically selected product in their text campaigns. The feature allows brands to configure product feeds once and have them automatically personalize across all text channels without additional setup. This brings the same dynamic product feed capability that existed for email to SMS and RCS channels.
