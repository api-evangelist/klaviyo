By Cyber Monday customers face message fatigue after days of Black Friday promotions, and this guide covers 13 SMS strategies for peak-season conversions, including using RCS for shoppable messages, reaching global customers via WhatsApp, focusing on engaged segments, and creating VIP experiences. It emphasizes a coordinated omnichannel approach, noting brands see an average of 19% GMV growth after consolidating email and SMS.
