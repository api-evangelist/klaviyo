Klaviyo launched a Sidekick app extension as a day-one partner with Shopify, letting merchants ask Sidekick questions about their Klaviyo CRM data directly within the Shopify Admin. The extension provides conversational access to campaign performance, flow analytics, segment data, and revenue attribution metrics without switching between platforms.
